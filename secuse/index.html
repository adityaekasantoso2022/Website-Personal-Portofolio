Secuse

Designing An Informative And Enjoyable Car Selection Process At Secuse

Industry

E-Commerce

Timeline

2022

Platform

Mobile Application

Scope

UX Design, UI Design, Prototype

About

Secuse is an application for selling and buying second used items.

You can find so many category of second used items from daily equipment to automotives. The superior feature is that users can compare the items that they want to buy.

Background

The automotive industry market has experienced an increase in the last few years.

With this data, Secuse sees an opportunity to maximize automotive sales and increase users on the application by creating an informative and enjoyable automotive selection and buying journey.

Challenge

Choosing a used car is quite difficult because there is a lot of things that must be considered.

We have to consider many things to ensure the car we choose is still in good condition and meets our needs, such as mileage, year of vehicle, service record, and others.

Strategy

Explore By Budget & Type

This feature help first time car buyer. Usually first time buyer looking for family car, so they can just choose the category and the budget range and we will show the recommendation.

Detailed Car Information

Buying second used car need to consider many things, so we present very detailed car information to user, so they know the exact condition of the car, this help users to screening the car.

Car Service History

One of the most important things when considering buying a used car is its service history. A car with a clear and good service record will make buyers feel safer and more comfortable.

Instalment Simulation

Currently, many people choose to buy cars in instalments because it is easier. We provide instalment simulations so that users can consider and choose which instalments suit their budget.

Item Comparison

One of the processes in choosing a used car is comparing one car with another. We provide a comparison feature so that it is easier for users to compare without going back and forth to open car details.
