Cassius

Designing A Seamless And Informative Shop Website For Cassius, A Daily Wellness Supplement

Industry

Supplement

Timeline

2023

Platform

Website

Scope

UX Design, UI Design, Prototype

About

Cassius is a wellness brand focused on providing high-quality, balanced nutritional products.

Cassius is a cutting-edge supplement that optimised physical and cognitive health with its natural formula. You can unlock your full potential and achieve peak performance in your activity

Background

Cassius wants to revamp their website to make more informative and have a high converting sales.

We sell supplement products so we need to educate users about the benefits and advantages of our supplements. Once users already understand the benefits of our supplements, it will be easier for them to decide to buy.

Challenge

How do we educate users about our supplements benefits and advantage.

We present information about the advantages of Cassius, why choose Cassius, natural ingredients Cassius, and others. So that customers become more trust and confident in consuming Cassius every day.

Strategy

Promotion Banner On Hero

We show promo banner on the hero section so users immediately know what promos are currently available. If there are lots of attractive promotions, it can attract buyers and increase sales.

Overall User Satisfaction

By displaying overall user satisfaction, we can reflect product quality from customer satisfaction reviews, so this can increase customer trust, which will also have an impact on sales.

Shop On First Landing Page

We display product purchase details on the first landing page, this is to create an efficient and seamless flow for customers to purchase our products.

User Review With Before & After Photo

We display user reviews with photos of the results before and after consuming our supplements, this can increase customer trust. Customer see real results from using our supplements.

Cassius Advantage

We show the advantages of Cassius supplements such as benefits for the body, complete nutrition, natural ingredients, and others. This can increase customer insight about our supplements.

Cassius Ingredients

Customers need to know what our supplements are made from, so we display the natural ingredients in our supplements, so that customers have more trust and feel safe when consume Cassius.
