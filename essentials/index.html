Essentials

Designing Modern Men's Fashion Marketplace With Curated Items From Fashion Expert

Industry

Fashion

Timeline

2023

Platform

Website

Scope

UX Design, UI Design, Prototype

About

Essentials is your go-to fashion marketplace for trendy clothing and accessories.

Connecting fashion lovers with curated styles from established and emerging designers. Essentials is here to make it easier for you to find the best fashion today.

Background

The fashion industry is a big and in-demand industry from ancient times.

Essentials saw a large market potential and decided to do business in this area. Essentials started by creating a fashion marketplace with the items are curated by fashion experts. This gives Essentials an advantage over other fashion marketplaces.

Challenge

Fashion trends move very fast every day, making it difficult for people to keep on the trend.

Essentials comes as fashion marketplace with items that have been curated by fashion experts. This makes it easier for buyers to follow current trends.

Strategy

Curated Items By Fashion Expert

The advantage of Essentials is that it helps buyers stay keep up with the current fashion trends. Essentials sells items that have been curated by fashion experts.

Clean And Elegant Touch

Essentials' target market is middle and upper class people who want to keep up with current fashion, so presenting a clean and elegant website is in line with its target market.

Black And White Color Scheme

Because we want to present a clean and elegant website, choosing black and white is very suitable. We avoid using too many colors, we just use black and white with an elegant layout.

Unique Layout

The common layout is too ordinary, we come up with a unique layout to attract buyers. A unique layout can provide a unique perspective and make buyers remember us.
